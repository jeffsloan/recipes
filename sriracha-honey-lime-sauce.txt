Ingredients:
 - 1 tablespoon of sriracha
 - 1 tablespoon of honey
 - 1 lime

Directions:
 1. Combine sriracha, honey, and freshly squeezed lime juice into small ramekin or bowl. Mix with a fork until smooth.

 2. Heat frying or sauce pan over medium heat.
 
 2. Pour sauce into pan and mix over heat until hot and slightly reduced.
 
 3. Serve over chicken, pork, or vegetables!
